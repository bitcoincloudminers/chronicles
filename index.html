The Evolution of Money

3000 BC - Barter System

Direct exchange of goods and services without a medium of exchange. Limited by the double coincidence of wants.

2000 BC - Commodity Money

Shells, cattle, and precious metals used as store of value. First standardized units of account.

700 BC - First Coins

Lydians created the first official currency made from electrum (gold-silver alloy). Government backing of money begins.

1000 AD - Paper Money

China introduces paper currency backed by gold reserves. Portability and convenience improved.

1971 - Nixon Shock

US ends gold standard. Fiat currency era begins with money backed only by government promise.

2008 - Bitcoin Genesis

Satoshi Nakamoto creates Bitcoin: the first decentralized, digital, sound money with a fixed supply of 21 million.

2024 - Institutional Adoption

Major corporations and institutions like BlackRock, MicroStrategy, and nation-states accumulate Bitcoin reserves.
